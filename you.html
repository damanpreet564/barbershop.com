POLICIES

Shop Policies

NO SHOWS

If you do not show up for your appointment you will be marked as a “no show” in our booking system. No shows are required to leave a credit card number when booking their next 5 appointments.

LATE APPOINTMENTS

If you are more than 5 minutes late for your appointment you may not be able to receive services. If you are late more than once you will be marked as a “no show”. No shows are required to leave a credit card number when booking their next 5 appointments.

CANCELLATIONS

You may cancel an appointment up to 2 hours in advance of your appointment time.

COVID-19

Covid-19 Safety

Before You Arrive

Wash your hair at home
Make sure you bring a face covering.

When You Arrive

Sign in at the table outside the shop
please wait outside (2 metres apart) or in your car until you are called
When you enter the shop, your temperature will be taken

Other Measures

Chairs and workstations have been arranged to maintain physical distancing
Our barbers wear face coverings, face shields and gloves for your protection
Equipment and workstations are sanitized between clients
